# Data structure notes

The data we will be storing consists of
- Chapters, which contain 
  - Pages

### Chapter
- Chapter title
- Pages included
- Previous, Next chapter
- May or may not show up in archive

### Page
- Page title
- Page date
- Images [with thumbnails]
- Previous, Next page
- Page Hover element  
- Parent chapter
- Blog post  
- Comments
